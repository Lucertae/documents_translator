AUTHORIZED DISTRIBUTION AGREEMENT

The undersigned:

1. MIMAKI EUROPE B.V., a company registered in (1112 AA) Diemen under the laws of the Netherlands, having its office at Stammerdijk 7/e, in this matter duly represented by Mr. Takahiro Hiraki, hereinafter to be referred to as: "Mimaki";
and

2. Mimaki Bompan Textile S.R.L., a company registered in Tradate under the laws of Italy, having its office at Via Europe 10, in this matter duly represented by Dr. Massimo Bompan and Mr. Ryosuke Nakayama, hereinafter to be referred to as: "Authorised Distributor";
also referred to below individually as "Party" and jointly as "Parties";

Whereas:

A Mimaki is a supplier of high-quality graphic computer devices, software and accessory products, and wishes to market and sell these products in Italy, Malta;

B Authorised Distributor is a company which is willing and capable to enter into this Agreement to market and sell these products from the allocated premises on the conditions set out below;

C Mimaki wishes Authorised Distributor to market and sell products in Authorised Distributor's own name and for Authorised Distributor's own account and risk under the terms and conditions set out below;

D Authorised Distributor acknowledges that it has received full prior information as required by law with regard to the Products, Authorised Distributor's obligations, the relevant market conditions, the Intellectual Property rights and any other information relevant to the performance of Authorised Distributor under this Agreement.

Now therefore:

The Parties have agreed to enter into this agreement, expressing their agreement upon the following provisions:

Article 1 – Definitions

Agreement
Shall mean this distribution agreement concluded between the Parties;

Authorised Distributor
Shall mean the counterpart of Mimaki under this Agreement;

Delivery
Shall mean delivery in accordance with delivery condition EXW warehouse ITG at Amsterdam or Mimaki Diemen (Incoterms® 2020);

Exhibits
Shall mean the exhibit listed in Article 23.6 of this Agreement, and such further exhibits as to be attached in accordance with Article 23.8 of this Agreement;

Incoterms® 2020
Shall mean Incoterms® 2020 as published by the International Chamber of Commerce, as amended from time to time;

Purchase Price
Shall mean the purchase price to be paid to Mimaki by Authorised Distributor for the Products, as described in the Price List;

Price List
Shall mean the Price List as made available to Authorised Distributor from time to time;

Products
Shall mean 1) graphic devices as marked on the Product List attached as Exhibit 1 to this Agreement, and made available to Authorised Distributor by Mimaki from time to time, and 2) the corresponding products (e.g. spare parts and/or inks) to these marked devices;

Mimaki
Shall mean Mimaki Europe B.V.;

Territory
Shall mean Italy, Malta;

Intellectual Property
Shall mean all intellectual property owned by Mimaki Engineering Co., Ltd. and its group companies.
